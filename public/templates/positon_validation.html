d.Functional &/or Technical Competencies: These are those competencies which are required for managing the role specific responsibilities (eg. Drilling & Blasting Operations for a Mine Manager, Technology Evaluation for a TCD personnel, Cyber Security for a IT Head, Treasury Management for a Head Finance etc).We along with the Consultants have identified competencies which would be required to achieve the KRAs.You are requested to

i.Delete/Add Competencies
ii. Check on the proficiency level (All these have been mapped on a 4 Level proficiency scale : Level 1 Knowing and Doing under supervision, Level 2 Doing without Supervision, Level 3: Developing & Improving, and Level 4; Integrating & Path Finding. For your convenience you may use the heuristic given in the following table

Level 1 Level 2 Level 3 Level 4
Knows (basic understanding) and can do under supervision Does independently without supervision Knows the system and can now improve on the same – in terms of technology and also processes Is an expert
Typically has theoretical knowledge and is aware of the SOPs/ Basic Process for that workflow Knows all the SOPs and is able to perform the same - including the critical ones such as start-up and shutdown Emergency procedures Improves on SOPs; Identifies new methods/ processes; suggests/makes changes to MOCs, processes, process parameters etc Knows the best practices or establishes the best practices.Knows what needs to be done to be the best in the industry

 

PLEASE NOTE: You are capturing the requirements of the JOB, not of yourself.You could well be at say Level 4 in a given area, but the job requires only Level 2 (for example).Kindly therefore capture Level 2 in the system.

iii. Check/Validate the Criticality Level: A competency can be either Critical or Important or Less Important.For example, a Competency of say Communication (for two people of the same level) could at different levels of criticality, for a Sales Person – Critical while for a Stores person, it is either Important or Less Important.

e.Managerial & Behavioral Competencies: These are competencies which are required for carrying out the Managerial role the job.The competencies shown, are based on a Adani Group Level model, and hence is more or less fixed in its contours.Please use the above (in given in point 'd') as guidelines for this as well.
